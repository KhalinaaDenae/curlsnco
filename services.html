New Clients

Clients new to our salon will book a one of a kind signature first time curly cut. At your first appointment, you'll receive a haircut that fits your face, lifestyle, and hair texture. Unlike most salons, we cut curly hair dry to shape the hair in it's natural texture!

After cutting, we'll give you an incredibly soothing detox wash to remove hard water and product buildup. Healthy hair is a priority for our team which is why we use organic haircare.

Then, we'll style your beautiful curls! The signature first time curly haircut is truly a unique experience because you receive one-on-one coaching from your stylist who will teach you how to care for and style your curls at home.

Your stylist will send you home with a curlscription card so you'll have an easy guide for recreating your style at home!
